About Us

Korean cuisine is the customary cooking traditions and practices of the culinary arts of Korea. Korean cuisine has evolved through centuries of social and political change. Originating from ancient agricultural and nomadic traditions in Korea and southern Manchuria, Korean cuisine has evolved through a complex interaction of the natural environment and different cultural trends Korea is famous for the food they serve. They believe food should satisfy all the senses, not just fill the stomach. They apply a balance between Yin and Yang in producing healthy and proportionate food, thus forming a beautiful and enjoyable art. Thats why we chose the name DamBam
